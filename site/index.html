Designed for modern freelancers

Ditch complex cloud platforms. InvoiceGen provides a minimal, distraction-free environment built entirely for macOS.

Local-first by design

Your database lives locally on your hard drive. No cloud sync failures, no accounts, no monthly subscription fees, and no online tracking.

Clients & Projects

Keep a unified list of clients and associate them with active projects. Set customizable hourly rates and keep tabs on budgets seamlessly.

Instant PDF Generation

Generate professional, print-ready PDF invoices in seconds. Customize business details, VAT, taxes, discounts, and payment notes locally.

Financial Dashboard

Track your yearly billing, unpaid invoice sums, client distributions, and outstanding amounts in a native SwiftUI analytics dashboard.
